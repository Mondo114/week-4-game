Crystal Collector

How to Play: The small crystals will be given a random value at the start of each round. Click on the small crystals to match the value of the big crystal.

Click on a crystal to reveal its value and add that value of points to your "Total Collected".

You win the game by matching your Total Collected to the value of the big crystal.

You lose the game if your Total Collected exceeds the value of the big crystal.
